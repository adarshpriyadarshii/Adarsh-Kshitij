Plans that fit your needs.

No matter the size of your budget, Kshitij.com has a plan that’s right for you. Create a free website and get access to professional themes, 3 GB of storage space, community support, and more.


If you need more features, you can choose from one of three affordable plans. With each plan you’ll get a custom domain name, more space for your files, and priority support. You asked, we answered!


  • You can pay for your brand new Kshitij.com plan, add-ons, and domains in the Store checkout using any major credit card, debit card, or PayPal.

  • Plans and domains renew annually and we take care of everything. We charge your account one month before the end of the subscription period. If your payment information needs updating, we’ll let you know.

  • Purchases made on Kshitij.com can be canceled and refunded during the refund period. You can also unsubscribe at any time if you don’t want your subscription to renew.

  • On Kshitij.com, we provide e-mail forwarding, but if you’d rather have full email hosting, you can connect another provider to your custom domain.

  • Premium themes are paid themes with exciting options for customization and exclusive support from theme authors themselves. Choose the Premium Plan or Business Plan for unlimited Premium Themes.
